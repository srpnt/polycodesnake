01 Overview
Incident Summary

A macro-triggered flash crash on Oct 10, 2025 (BTC -14%, Gas >500 gwei) caused cascading liquidations across DeFi. Morpho's market isolation architecture contained contagion at the protocol level, but two MEV Capital curated USDC vaults realized bad debt due to collateral depeg events in exotic stablecoin markets (xUSD and sdeUSD).

While there is not a direct causal effect between Oct 10 drop and bad debt that occured on these two MEV Capital vaults, it is clear that the general market uncertainty, and sudden price drops have broken various market structures including the strategies these vaults have previously relied on. This research assumes, with high confidence, that there are indirect links between the macro flash crash, leverage unwinds, and exotic stablecoin depegs.

Protocol functionality remained intact. Losses were socialized to vault depositors - no DAO or protocol-level funds were impacted.

Key Finding

Both failures share the same root cause: exotic stablecoin collaterals that faced insufficient secondary market liquidity were whitelisted in curator supply queues without adequate exit plans or liquidity. When the depegs occurred, liquidations were either unprofitable or impossible at any meaningful recovery rate.

Important, even though obvious, to note is that this is a curator risk parameter failure, not a protocol failure.

05 SQL Query
Dune Query - Realized Bad Debt Events (Octâ€“Nov 2025)

Purpose: Identify bad debt events for MEV Capital USDC vaults during the crash window by scanning MorphoBlue_evt_Liquidate for non-zero badDebtAssets, joined with market creation events to extract collateral token addresses (used to filter for xUSD, sdeUSD, and related markets).

-- Realized Bad Debt on Morpho Blue (Octâ€“Nov 2025)
-- Filters for liquidations where badDebtAssets > 0
-- Joins CreateMarket to identify collateral token per market

WITH bad_debt_events AS (
  SELECT
    l.evt_block_time,
    l.evt_tx_hash,
    l.id                        AS market_id,
    l.badDebtAssets / 1e6       AS bad_debt_usdc_amount, -- USDC (6 decimals)
    l.badDebtShares,
    m.marketParams
  FROM morpho_blue_ethereum."MorphoBlue_evt_Liquidate" l
  LEFT JOIN morpho_blue_ethereum."MorphoBlue_evt_CreateMarket" m
    ON l.id = m.id
  WHERE l.evt_block_time >= TIMESTAMP '2025-10-10 00:00:00'
    AND l.evt_block_time <= TIMESTAMP '2025-11-15 23:59:59'
    AND l.badDebtAssets > 0
)

SELECT
  b.evt_block_time,
  b.market_id,
  json_extract_scalar(b.marketParams, '$.collateralToken') AS collateral_token,
  b.bad_debt_usdc_amount,
  b.evt_tx_hash
FROM bad_debt_events b
ORDER BY b.evt_block_time DESC;
Query Notes
FieldNotes
badDebtAssets / 1e6Normalizes raw on-chain units to human-readable USDC amount (6 decimal token)
badDebtAssets > 0Key filter - in a healthy liquidation this is zero. Non-zero = protocol absorbed a loss
json_extract_scalar(...collateralToken)Pulls collateral address from marketParams JSON - use this to identify xUSD / sdeUSD markets
LEFT JOIN CreateMarketRequired to access marketParams - not available directly on the Liquidate event
ORDER BY evt_block_time DESCMost recent events first - useful for triage during a live incident
